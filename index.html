My hometown

I grew up in Kaua'i, Hawai'i and I am proud to represent that where ever I go. If you haven't been there before to the average person it is like paradise and to some extent it is. But to most locals it is home and we love it. The culture of there is nothing that I have ever experienced here in the mainland. There isn't as much opportunity there compared to here but I would still go back in a heartbeat. The people you meet are 90% always showing grace and kindness to you and it is just a great place to be. In all of the great things that Hawai'i brings, there is one thing that tops all else and that is the food, now you can get a lunch plate for under $10 with a drink included, some spots even under $5.
